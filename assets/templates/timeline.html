oct 1, 2016

Loopring concept born

With his prior-experience in centralized exchange, Daniel was determined to solve the outstanding issues in centralized exchange using blockchain and smart contract technology.

Dec 1, 2016

Ring-matching concept verified

Daniel simulated his ring-matching idea and on-chain trade settlement process using Scala.

Jun 15, 2017

Whitepapers published

Daniel, Jay, and Alex published the first version of the Loopring protocol whitepaper in Chinese - under the name "Multilateral Token Trade Protocol (MTTP)".

Jun 22, 2017

Renamed to Loopring

We founded the Loopring Foundation, switched to use Loopring as the protocol/project name, and started to use the https://loopring.org domain.

Aug 1, 2017

Token sale kicked off

We launched our token sale using Ethereum smart contracts. The soft and hard cap for our token sale were 50,000 and 120,000 Ether, respectively.

Aug 16, 2017

Token sale concluded successfully

We raised 120,000 Ether and issued 1,374,956,262 LRC in total, 50% of LRC were distributed to our token holders. ICO price: 0.000175 ETH/LRC

Sept 2, 2017

LRC listed on Binance

Binance added two trading pairs: LRC/ETH, LRC/BTC to enable LRC trading.

Sept 4, 2017

Ether Raised Refunded

Following China's new regulatory requirements, the Loopring foundation completed all refund requests.

Dec 9, 2017

Protocol 1.0 deployed on Ethereum

We have previously deployed multiple pre-release versions on Ethereum mainnet, and on December 9th, we finally deployed 1.0 version.

Dec 28, 2017

Loopr Wallet MVP Launched

Loopr (https://loopr.io) is a web-based non-custodial wallet that integrates with Loopring protocol. This MVP demonstrated Loopring Protocol's order matching and settlement mechanisms.

Dec 31, 2017

2018 Roadmap Released

We released Loopring 2018 Dev Roadmap

Jan 6, 2018

Protocol 1.0 deployed on Qtum

The Loopring protocol smart contracts were deployed on Qtum mainnet.

Jan 15, 2018

Joined Berkeley's Cross-chain Research

Loopring Joined the research initiative led by professor Alessandro Chiesa at UC Berkeley.

Feb 11, 2018

Dual-Authoring Patented & Published

Dual Authoring is an anti front-running technology to prevent orders and rings from being stolen by middlemen or block miners.

Feb 19, 2018

LEAF founded

Loopring Ecosystem Advancement Fund, or LEAF, is an umbrella fund. Each of its sub-fund is dedicated to one of our target blockchains that the Loopring Protocol has been or will be deployed onto.

Mar 9, 2018

LRC listed on Bittrex

Bittrex is a top-tier crypto-trading platform based and fully regulated in the US. To qualify the listing, the LRC token has successfully passed the Howey Test.

Mar 18, 2018

New Whitepaper Released

We released a new whitepaper details the design and implementation of Loopring protocol on Ethereum.

Apr 30, 2018

Loopr Beta3 Launched

We redesigned Loopr's UX and UI and launched multiple beta versions. Loopr is now fully functional and works as expected.

May 1, 2018

LRN Listed on 5 exchanges

LRN, Loopring protocol token on NEO, were listed on gate, cex, syex.io, idax, and bitpaction.

May 16, 2018

LRC Listed on Bithumb

LRC was listed by Bithumb, one of the largest crypto-exchanges world-wide.

Jun 24, 2018

Loopring Deployed on NEO Testnet

We have deployed the first prototype of the Loopring Protocol on NEO testnet.

Jun 28, 2018

Developer Portal Launched

We collected a set of documents for community developers to better understand and use our protocol and open-sourced projects.

Sept 08, 2018

Loopring Protocol V2 Fee Model Unveiled

In this article we explained the new fee model in upcoming protocol v2 release.

We also release a new whitepaper.

Oct 18, 2018

Loopring Joined DeFi

Loopring Joins #DeFi to build up the decentralized financial system.

Oct 28, 2018

Announced Bug and Optimization Bounty for Smart Contracts 2.0

We set up big rewards for people who will enhance our protocol code quality

Nov 07, 2018

PwC Hong Kong collaborates with Loopring Foundation

Loopring and PwC started a joint initiative to explore standards and practices to support future security tokens and stablecoins.

Mar 09, 2019

Relase Oedax Paper

Oedax is Loopring's new auction based trading protocol that allows both buyers and sellers to participate after an auction starts.
